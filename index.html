WELCOME TO MY GALLERY

Artist: Razan Dahyan

Music: Cayendo - Frank Ocean

Hello, I'm so happy you joined my "Flow of Illusion" galley! This is meant to be a relaxing place that displays a colourful, modern experience. Multiple different shapes will be displayed and will give you the ability to contol each art piece! Please take the time to enjoy a 3 minute experience with one of my favourite songs!

Note: Press play if the song did not start. You can adjust the audio however you please.

RAINBOW LINES

This piece has multiple curvy lines with different coloured gradiantes that move together until they have reached a stop. The lines can be pause when right clicking.

A WAVE OF COLOURS

This piece is a cubic shaped wave that continuously moves up and down vertically. When right clicking, another slightly rotated wave appears with a different color creating a mesmerizing and relaxing illusion.

COLOURFUL BUBBLES

This piece consists of a group of bubbles that follow your mouse. Once clicked on the screen, the screen turns white and the strokes change colors.

Thank you for being part of my gallery!

It was a pleaseure to have you experience my work and interpret it however the piece speaks to you!
